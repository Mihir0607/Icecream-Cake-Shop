We want to make sure our ice creams provide maximum happiness and nostalgia! Everyone who comes to Naturals for the first time ends up getting the Tender Coconut Ice Cream – this eternal favourite is absolutely comforting. Indian summers are not easy but when you have our Tender Coconut Ice Cream by your side, it definitely provides the respite we all need. The quintessential seasonal flavour

As the days begin to cool and the monsoon showers bring relief from the summer heat, enjoying a refreshing treat becomes even more delightful. Naturals Lychee Ice Cream represents the spirit of this seasonal change, presenting a flavour as refreshing as the first rains after a long summer. Lychee, a fruit synonymous with the tropical season, boasts a unique blend of juicy sweetness and subtle floral notes. This delicate fruit is transformed by Naturals into a creamy, delicious ice cream that beautifully captures the essence of the season – evoking images of lush, rain-kissed landscapes and leisurely afternoons. Handcrafted with care One of the standout qualities of Naturals Lychee Ice Cream is the meticulous care taken in its preparation. The lychees are deseeded by hand, ensuring that each piece retains its integrity and flavor before being made into ice cream. This careful process ensures that every bite of our lychee ice cream contains delightful pieces of lychee, enhancing the texture and taste of the treat.

Refreshing and thirst-quenching

The moment you take a spoonful of Naturals Lychee Ice Cream, you’re greeted with a burst of cold, refreshing flavour that instantly revitalizes you. The ice cream melts smoothly, releasing the fresh, juicy taste of lychee that satiates your taste buds like nothing else. In fact, Naturals Lychee Ice Cream is not just a dessert; it’s a delightful antidote to the fluctuating weather. Its enlivening properties make it perfect for those days when the weather is unpredictable, offering a delicate balance that refreshes the palate without overwhelming it.
