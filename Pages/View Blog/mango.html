Ah, the arrival of summer! The season of scorching heat, lazy afternoons, and of course, the sweet anticipation of indulging in some delicious ice cream. And what better flavour to celebrate summer’s arrival than the king of fruits itself – mango. Among the many pleasures of this holiday season, few things evoke the essence of summer quite like a scoop of delicious mango ice cream. Besides, when it comes to indulging in this fruity delight, Naturals Ice Cream takes the top position in good taste and quality. In this blog, we’ll deep-dive into the world of Naturals’ Mango Ice Cream, and explore its rich flavour, innovative offerings, and other creative ways to enjoy it.

Buggus’ classic mango ice cream

Let’s start with the classic—the timeless ice cream flavour that has captured the hearts of mango lovers for generations. Naturals’ Mango Ice Cream is crafted from the finest Alphonso mangoes sourced directly from the best mango orchards in Ratnagiri. Which is why, each spoonful of this delicious ice cream is laced with a creamy texture and intense mango flavour, capable enough to transport you to those sun-drenched orchards with every bite. Whether savoured on its own or paired with a fruit, this iconic ice cream never fails to evoke feelings of nostalgia and pure joy.

Creative ways to enjoy:

But why stop at scooping it straight out of the tub? Naturals’ Mango Ice Cream invites all kinds of culinary creativity, offering endless possibilities for indulgence. One delightful way to enjoy it is by whipping up a palatable mango milkshake. Simply blend a few scoops of mango ice cream with milk until smooth and creamy, and voila! You have a decadent beverage that’s perfect for sipping on a hot summer day. For an extra touch of indulgence, top it off with whipped cream and a sprinkle of chopped nuts or luscious mango chunks.

If milkshakes aren’t your thing, fear not—there are plenty of other ways to enjoy Naturals’ Mango Ice Cream. Get creative in the kitchen and use it to make mango ice cream sandwiches, mango floats, or even mango ice cream sundaes with your favourite toppings. The possibilities are endless, limited only by your imagination.
