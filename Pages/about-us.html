Buggu's Ice Cream Shop: A Sweet History

Buggus Ice Cream Shop was started by
Martha and George Buggus in 1985 in a small
Vermont town.

They loved making homemade ice cream
and opened a shop with classic flavors
like vanilla and chocolate.

Over time, they added unique flavors like
Maple Bacon and became a local favorite.
